The Nexus, 󱦐󱤌󱤩󱦑

The Nexus is the name of the current Headspace we have. It's structurally complicated, architecturally impossible, and pretty cool to look at with your eyes (or I guess whatever you use to look at stuff in the headspace).
󱤑󱦐󱤮󱤺󱥺󱦑 has made a drawing of the headspace (which, like the real thing, is forever changing). Here it is, with annotations for a better understanding

  • #8b8a9c
    The moon. It's not actually a moon, as in it doesn't orbit anything and has no effect on anything, but it's very pretty to look at. You can even go up there.
  • #5c448d
    The moon base. It's a wizard-y structure with a very long spiral staircase that takes you to an observatory where you can look at stars that don't actually exist. A pretty fun activity!
  • #d2d1b3
    󱦁󱦐󱥪󱥦󱦑's house. It's pretty far from the other houses, but he doesn't mind as he can just teleport to them. It's in the shape of a skull and no one - not even him - knows why. It was chosen at random while scrolling through pinterest.
  • #5153bc
    󱤑󱦐󱤮󱤺󱥺󱦑's and 󱤑󱦐󱥎󱤾󱦑's houses. They're the same as they were in the Ocean. This time they're in a pond, which while less exciting makes the presence of all the insects make slightly more sense.
